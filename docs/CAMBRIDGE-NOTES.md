# Cambridge Forge

This document defines the scope and approach of the Cambridge Forge section.

## Scope

Cambridge Forge covers:

- B2 First;
- C1 Advanced;
- C2 Proficiency.

It does not cover TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo English Test, or other exams.

Keeping the scope narrow keeps the project focused and accurate.

## Purpose

Cambridge Forge is orientation and study strategy.

It helps learners understand:

- what each exam level represents;
- what skills are assessed;
- how to prepare with method;
- how Codex pages connect to exam needs.

It is not:

- a source of official practice tests;
- a reproduction of Cambridge material;
- a guarantee of a result;
- a replacement for official handbooks, sample papers, or preparation books.

## Exam page structure

Each exam page should include:

1. **Overview** — what the exam certifies.
2. **Structure** — general papers and skill areas.
3. **Skills assessed** — reading, writing, listening, speaking, and use of English where relevant.
4. **Task types** — described in general terms.
5. **Study strategy** — how to prepare consistently.
6. **Common mistakes** — what candidates often do wrong.
7. **Routine** — how to plan study before the exam.
8. **Codex links** — which grammar, writing, vocabulary, or speaking pages support the exam.

All explanations must be original.

## Connection to the Codex

Cambridge Forge should point learners back to the rest of the project.

Examples:

- B2 First connects to B2 grammar, essay structure, and argumentation.
- C1 Advanced connects to inversion, hedging, register, and advanced linking.
- C2 Proficiency connects to rhetorical style, idiomatic precision, implication, and sophisticated writing.

Forge tells the learner what the exam asks for. The Codex teaches the language needed to answer it.

## Tone

The tone should be calm, practical, and realistic.

Exam preparation can make learners anxious. The writing should reduce confusion, not sell fear or miracle tricks.

## Accuracy

Cambridge exam formats can change.

Any specific claim about exam structure should be checked against current official Cambridge material before publication. Official Cambridge English sources are the final authority.
